About Me

Hello, my name is Kyra Davis. I am a native to Nashville, and I am on the journey to becoming a web developer. I attended Vanderbilt University and graduated with a bachelor's in Psychology (Research Concentration). After graduating, I spent two years in Gunma, Japan teaching English to junior high school students.

After returning to the United States, I joined a startup tech company. The company's main focus was perfecting their app and website for patients and caregivers so that they could easily manage several medications in one place. As a Care Advisor, I assisted patients with their experience with our products and services. By working closely with the developer team, my interest in web app development began to spark.

I am currently learning coding and full stack web development in the Vanderbilt Coding Bootcamp. It is a challenge, but it is also very exciting. There is always so much more to learn and add to my coding toolbox!
